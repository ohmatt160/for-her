She’s a cute girl. A very cute one! I can't help the fact that she is very cute! Fair-skinned (I like fair girls!), slim, with a smile that makes her even more adorable, especially when she's shy.

We started talking online on Facebook as casual friends. I think I eventually asked for her WhatsApp number and added her, but we didn’t talk much after that because she wasn’t often online. One day, out of the blue, she sent me a video of myself that my brother had posted on TikTok. She asked, 'Is this you?' I said yes, and she complimented the video, saying she liked it. Then, she called me on video, but all she did was smile without saying much – she was cute, though. After that call, we started chatting more regularly. At the time, I was in school, but when I got home, I made sure to go see her with some friends. It was after that first meet-up that our friendship really began to grow. My brother, friends, and I would go to her place at night to hang out and chat.

As I already mentioned — Facebook to WhatsApp to TikTok to calls to friendship.

She doesn't have a bad attitude, though she can be dramatic sometimes!
